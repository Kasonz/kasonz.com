Please Don't Say Just Hello/Hi In Chat

Trying to be polite by not jumping right into the questions, like we would do in person or on the phone.

But Chat is neither of those things, typing is much slower than talking - and I'm not a fast typer ðŸ˜…

Instead of being polite, you are just making the other person wait for you to phrase your question, which is lost productivity.

Focus on efficiency: "For a faster response, feel free to jump right into your question or request."

Highlight clarity: "To best assist you, please include your question or request with your initial message."

Offer context: "If you'd like to check if I'm available first, you can say 'Hey Kason, quick question about...' "

Moreover, posing your question before awaiting a response enables asynchronous communication. This means that if the other party is unavailable and you leave before their return, they can still respond to your inquiry rather than simply encountering a "Hello" and wondering what they missed.
